Amazing Uma Mini Features
Intuitive Controls

A touch sensitive, full range volume dial with dual light level control makes Uma Mini super tactile and simple to use in any occasion. Volume and music selection can also be managed from any mobile device via Bluetooth with up to 20 hours of wireless play per charge.

Party Mode

Uma Mini is all about connecting spaces with light and music, allowing you to pair up 24 Minis from a single mobile device to make any place a party. Its perfect for indoor and outdoor and settings at home, lounge areas and hospitality settings alike.
